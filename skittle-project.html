Conclusion

The purple skittles, or grape skittles have the most mass at 1.04 grams. The grape skittles also have the highest density
at 1.48571 g/mL. Yellow or lemon and green or lime are tied for lowest mass at 1.005 grams. Lime skittles have the lowest
density at 1.41055 g/mL.
